# VANISHED — Design Document

## Concept
A mystery-detective game set during a school field trip where 17-year-old Emily Carter disappears. Players race against a 6-hour countdown to find her before dawn.

## Core Mechanic
Investigation loop: explore locations → collect clues → interview suspects (trust-gated dialogue) → connect evidence on Deduction Board → accuse + rescue. Every action costs time.

## Art Style
Dark cinematic thriller aesthetic — rain, dim lighting, heritage hotel and mountain forest settings. Generated AI backgrounds with overlaid UI.
